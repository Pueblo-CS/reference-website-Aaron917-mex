Basketball was invented in Springfield, Massachusetts, by James Naismith in 1891. It was a less injury prone sport then football.

Basketball needs two teams with five player on each team.Score by shooting ball threw the hoop, you move the ball by passing and dribbling.

A team is supposed to score more points then the opposing team. Points inside the arc are worth 2 points. Point outside the arc are worth 3 points.

The game consist of 4 quater each lasting 12 minutes. The offense must score before the shot clock runs out.

If the ball does not hit the rim before the shot clocks runs out it is a turn over and the opposing teams ball.
